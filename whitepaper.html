● What is Tai Chi?
Tai Chi is a philosophical concept in Chinese culture，Black and white, day and night, yin and yang, Bear and Bull, they operate alternately to maintain balance. This philosophy has been applied in Chinese Kung Fu, business models, and other fields. there are many practitioners of Tai Chi all over the world. Alibaba's Jack Ma, movie star Jet Li, etc. are all Tai Chi fans and admirers.
