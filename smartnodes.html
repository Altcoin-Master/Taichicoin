● Smart nodes are similar to POS, locking a certain amount of coins in your own wallet to get rewards.

● If you don't have a VPS, you can set up a virtual node. (But keep an eye out for Discord announcements, we can ban virtual nodes at any time.)
https://github.com/Altcoin-Master/Taichicoin/tree/main/doc/smartnodes#local-wallet-setup

● If you have a spare VPS, you can set up a real node. 1 CPU, 512M memory, and open port 21003. (This step is voluntary, we don't want people spending extra money to buy a VPS)
https://github.com/Altcoin-Master/Taichicoin/tree/main/doc/smartnodes

● As the taichicoin network matures Smartnode collateral ramps up and you will need to recreate your Smartnode in order to increase the collateral amount.
https://github.com/Altcoin-Master/Taichicoin/blob/main/doc/smartnodes/maintenance.md#increase-smartnode-collateral
